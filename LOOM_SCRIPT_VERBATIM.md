And Strategic Value - thirty points max - deal size potential.

*[0:47 - Click Tier 1 filter, then FQHC segment filter]*

The system applies different scoring logic for different practice types - Ambulatory versus Behavioral Health - and I can filter by tier, segment, or verified status.

*[0:57 - Click on top FQHC lead like "Grace Health, Inc." - drawer opens]*

Let's look at a real example. Grace Health scored eighty-nine out of one hundred.

*[1:02 - Point to Strategic Brief at top]*

The Strategic Intelligence Brief instantly shows me: estimated revenue lift of [READ THE ACTUAL NUMBER] dollars - and notice it says VERIFIED, meaning this is calculated from actual Medicare claims, not projected.

*[1:14 - Scroll to Score Breakdown section]*

The score is fully explainable. Pain: [READ ACTUAL NUMBER] out of forty points, measured via undercoding ratio from CMS data.

Fit: [READ ACTUAL NUMBER] out of thirty, FQHC designation plus MIPS and HPSA indicators.

Strategy: [READ ACTUAL NUMBER] out of thirty, based on patient volume and revenue scale.

*[1:28 - Hover over Pain bar to show tooltip]*

Hover over any component to see the exact reasoning - this is data-driven, not guesswork.

*[1:33 - Scroll down quickly to show contact info, then click Export button]*

The database includes contact info, network details, key metrics, buying signals - and I can export directly to HubSpot or CSV for immediate sales follow-up.

*[1:43 - Close drawer, back to filtered Tier 1 table]*

Bottom line: this tool transforms GTM from spray-and-pray to surgical precision.

Instead of manually researching thousands of clinics, sales now has [READ TIER 1 COUNT] high-priority leads with verified revenue opportunities, data-driven prioritization, and explainable intelligence - a multi-million dollar pipeline ready for immediate outreach.

*[1:58 - Smile, confident pause]*

Thanks for watching.

## IF YOU MESS UP

### Common Stumbles & Fixes

**Stumble**: "one-point-four... uh, million"
**Fix**: Just keep going, it's fine

**Stumble**: Forget to hover over tooltip
**Fix**: Hover when you say "explainable" (1:28) instead

**Stumble**: Can't find Export button
**Fix**: Skip it, go straight to closing (1:43)

**Stumble**: Lose your place in script
**Fix**: Jump to closing: "Bottom line, this tool transforms GTM..."
